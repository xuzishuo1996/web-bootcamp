2019.07 - 2019.09 SoC Group, IC Research and Development Department, SenseTime Technology

Project: AI Accelerator of Face Recognition and Feature Extraction from Video Streams

My responsibilities

  1. Programmed on RISC-V MCU (with C): the MCU communicated with the master controller ARM through IPC channels and received instructions from the ARM. According to the type and stage of the neural network parsed form instructions, it allocated spaces for input and output images and configured parameters on the external DDR. To forward the neural network, it transferred offline instructions and inputs that were saved on the DDR to the hardware processing unit. Finally, it moved the results back to the DDR using the DMA approach. Implemented 4 types of networks.
  2. Wrote Linux shell scripts for testing and tested programs on the hardware platforms.
  3. Measured the execution time of networks with timers. Analyzed and improved the performance.
